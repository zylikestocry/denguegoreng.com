What is Dengue?

Dengue is a viral disease transmitted by Aedes mosquitoes. It causes flu-like symptoms, including high fever, severe headaches, joint pain, and rash. In severe cases, it can lead to dengue hemorrhagic fever, which can be fatal.

It is important to understand how dengue spreads and how we can all work together to prevent it.

Dengue Cases in Recent Years

The number of dengue cases has been increasing globally. In 2023, over 5 million cases were reported worldwide. It's crucial to take preventive actions now to reduce these numbers.

Stay informed and take action. Together, we can fight dengue!
