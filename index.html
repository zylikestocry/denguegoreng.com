What is Dengue?

Dengue is a viral disease transmitted by Aedes mosquitoes. It causes flu-like symptoms, including high fever, severe headaches, joint pain, and rash. In some cases, it can lead to dengue hemorrhagic fever, which is a severe form of the disease.

Prevention Tips

  • Remove stagnant water from containers like flower pots, tires, and buckets.
  • Cover water storage containers to prevent mosquito breeding.
  • Wear long-sleeve shirts and pants to avoid mosquito bites.
  • Use insect repellent, especially during peak mosquito activity (dawn and dusk).
  • Keep windows and doors closed or use mosquito nets to protect indoors.
